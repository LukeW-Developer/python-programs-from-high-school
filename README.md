# Python High School Projects

## Overview

A collection of Python programs independently developed during High School to strengthen problem-solving abilities and explore practical programming applications related to Mathematics, Physics, and Computer Science.

These projects were primarily created during personal study time outside of formal coursework.

---

## Included Programs

### Physics Calculator

A program capable of calculating:

* Mass
* Energy
* Specific latent heat

Developed to support understanding of Physics concepts and formula implementation in Python.

---

### Compound & Simple Interest Calculator

A financial calculator capable of:

* Compound interest calculations
* Simple interest calculations
* Custom numerical inputs

Developed in relation to Mathematics studies and numerical problem-solving.

---

### Hexadecimal to Decimal Converter

A utility program designed to convert hexadecimal values into decimal numbers.

Developed to improve understanding of number systems and Computer Science concepts.

---

### Decimal to Hexadecimal Converter

An experimental converter program for converting decimal values into hexadecimal numbers.

This project remains partially unfinished and was developed as part of experimenting with number system conversion logic.

---

* Mathematical Calculations
* Problem-Solving Utilities
